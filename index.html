Problem Statement

Travel planning is often time-consuming and confusing due to scattered information, lack of personalization, and difficulty in comparing options.

Why it matters
  • Users waste time visiting multiple sites.
  • Options are hard to compare (price, duration, amenities).
  • No simple way to create/share personalized itineraries.

Project Overview

A web-based travel planner that provides personalized itineraries, destination insights, and booking assistance to make travel planning easier.

End Users

  • Tourists & Travelers
  • Travel Agencies
  • Frequent Business Travelers
  • Students planning trips

Tools & Techniques

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React
  • Backend: Node.js, Express.js
  • Database: MongoDB
  • APIs: Google Maps, Weather API
  • Hosting: GitHub Pages / Netlify
